Who are the forecasters? They are being listed as 'Service A' and 'Service B' for now. It can be said that A's forecasts are being broadcasted in the Portland Area and thus involve a human being, where B is an automated weather forecasting service.

Where is temperature being measured? Minimum and Maximum daily temperatures are being measured at Portland International Airport, which is also the location where the forecasts are predicting the temperature.

How is forecast error calculated? Weather Maniac archives 7-day (Service A) and 5-day (Service B) forecasts on the day they are made, and compares these predictions with the actual High and Low temperatures when they become available (the day after they occur).

What is Bias? In short, bias is a consistent offset error in forecasts, the most common example being Wet Bias where rainfall amount is consistently over forecasted.

What is Error StDev? This reflects how the forecast error varies from forecast to forecast, and thus is a measurement of how random the error is (and thus is hard to correct.)
