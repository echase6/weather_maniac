Follow the links to find out what the temperature will really be, with some level of confidence.

Since temperature forecasting is an inexact science, historical predictions have been analyzed to find out what error to expect, given the number of days in-advance a prediction is made.

Due to the statistical nature of these errors, confidence-levels are the best option. Choose between 50% and 99% to get a feel for what to expect, understanding that 100% certainty is an impossibility.
