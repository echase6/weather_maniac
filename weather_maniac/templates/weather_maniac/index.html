Weather Maniac seeks to inform you about how much confidence you should have in a temperature forecast. For example, when you are told that it will be as warm as 80° this coming Saturday, can you confidently believe it will be between 78° and 82°? Or maybe 75°-85°?

Historical predictions from a few forecasters have been compared with the actual measure temperatures to find out how far off they typically are, and the results have been summarized into a model that tells what to expect in the future.

Weather Maniac cannot tell you exactly what temperature to expect, but using this information it will tell you what to expect with 50% confidence, and with 90% confidence. See the Temperature Prediction page to see more.

To learn more about the temperature forecasting services, see the Statistics page. Unfortunately, the sources cannot be identified.
